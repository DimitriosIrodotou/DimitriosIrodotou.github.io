<h1 align="center">About</h1>

The Aquila Project compares the results of cosmological gas-dynamical simulations of galaxy formation within
a Milky Way-sized 􏰀CDM halo. The various runs differ in their numerical hydrodynamical treatment but share the same
initial conditions

The Aquila Project consists of 13 different gas-dynamical simulations of the formation of a galaxy in a ΛCDM halo of
similar mass to that of the Milky Way. Nine different codes were used for the project (two codes were run three
times each with different sub-grid physics modules). The various codes differ in their hydrodynamical technique (SPH
, AMR, moving-mesh): seven codes use the SPH technique, six of which are based on gadget and one on gasoline.
