<h1 align="center">Data</h1>

The initial data release for “The VIRGO Project” is documented in
[Frenk et al. 2000](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2000astro.ph..7362F/abstract){:target="_blank"}.
For snapshot outputs at 10 different redshifts for all the 4 models produced by the VIRGO project please visit 
[MPA's](https://wwwmpa.mpa-garching.mpg.de/Virgo/data_download.html#VIRGO){:target="_blank"}
project data page or contact
[Adrian Jenkins](http://star-www.dur.ac.uk/~arj/){:target="_blank"}.
