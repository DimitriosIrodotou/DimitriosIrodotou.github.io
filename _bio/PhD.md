Since 2017 I am a Ph.D. candidate and doctoral tutor at the University of Sussex. My scientific supervisors are Prof. 
Peter Thomas and Dr. Mark Sargent. 

My research so far has focussed on the dynamics of simulated galaxies and their components, mainly their discs, 
bulges and bars. I have developed and worked with semi-analytic models (L-Galaxies model) and cosmological and zoom-in 
simulations (the EAGLE and FLARES simulations). I am currently collaborating with the Auriga team at MPA, Garching 
where we study the effect of AGN feedback on disc and bulge dynamics in the Milky-Way analogue galaxies of the Auriga
simulation.
