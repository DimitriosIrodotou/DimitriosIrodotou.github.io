Welcome,

my name is Dimitrios Irodotou and I am a PhD student at the University of Sussex in Brighton, United Kingdom.

I research the formation and evolution of simulated galaxies using theoretical tools, such as the L-Galaxies semi-analytic model, the EAGLE cosmological simulation, the FLARES suite of zoom-in re-simulations and the Auriga suite of high-resolution magneto-hydrodynamical simulations of Milky Way-sized galaxies.
